About

Source 4 Industries was established in 1972. Since that beginning, we have become a trusted source for Material Handling Equipment and Industrial supplies. Though we initially opened our doors under the name of Bearing Sales, with one employee, we began our business as a family owned supplier of bearings and power transmission products. Within a few short years we expanded our product lines to include many industrial products and a full line of material handling equipment. Through years of expansion and the support of many customers and suppliers, we decided to change our business name to one that more accurately portrayed who we are and what we do. Therefore on January 1, 1996, we changed our company name to Source 4 Industries.
